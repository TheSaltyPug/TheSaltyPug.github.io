Projects/1.14/Pet Shop

Want to give your pet to another player, or maybe even sell them in a pet shop?
Well, now you can! Just install the Pet Shop data pack. To transfer a pet to another player, simply
drop the head of the player you want to give it to onto the pet along with the pet's
taming item and a poppy. Because love <3

Requires my PvP Heads data pack or any suitable replacement.
